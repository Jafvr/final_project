HISTORY OF THE BUSINESS

The business was created in 1940 to celebrate and break gender norms, at a time where women were subjected to a steriotypical role in society & men were considered to be manly and 'be the man of the house', Malik Models decided to go against the grain and let people break free from the shackles of society.
